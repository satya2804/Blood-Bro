What is this all about ?


We solve the problem of blood emergencies by connecting
blood donors directly with people in blood need.

What we do ?

We connect blood donors with recipients, without any intermediary such as blood banks, for an efficient and seamless process.


Innovative

Blood Bro is an innovative approach to address global health.We provide immediate access to blood donors.

Network

Blood Bro is one of several community organizations working together as a network that responds to emergencies in an efficient manner.

Get notified

Blood Bro Connect works with network partners to connect blood donors and recipients through an automated SMS service and a mobile app.

Totally Free

Blood Bro's ultimate goal is to provide an easy -to-use, easy-to-access, fast, efficient, and reliable way to get life-saving blood, totally Free of cost.

Save Life

We are a non profit foundation and our main objective is to make sure that everything is done to protect vulnerable persons.Help us by making a gift!

Our Hero's

Our organization relies heavily on volunteers, who constitute 96% of our entire
 workforce and sustain our humanitarian efforts. Blood donation is beneficial,
 and our volunteers are available around the clock to assist and donate blood. While blood banks store blood
 bags, our volunteers are present in real-time during emergencies for blood transfusions.
