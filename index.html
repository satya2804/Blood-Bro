How is this helpful ?


We can solve the blood emergency problems by connecting
blood donors directly with people who are in need of blood.

What we actually do?

We connect blood donors with recipients, without any intermediary such as blood banks, for an efficient and seamless process.


Innovatively

Blood Bro is an innovative approach for addressing health to all. We always support to blood donors.

Connections

Blood Bro is one big community organization working together as a chain network that responds to emergencies in a helpful manner.

Notification

Blood Bro works with network partners to connect blood donors and needed one through an automated SMS service and a mobile app.

It's not for money

Blood Bro's ultimate goal is to ensure an accessible, user-friendly, rapid, and dependable method for acquiring life-saving blood, completely free of charge..
